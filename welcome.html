Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CUPT) is an academic-research institution with excellence in the area of information technology. It gives primary emphasis on engineering, also committed to provide professional education in science, management, liberal arts and other areas of its demonstrated competence. The university is located at Nanshan tourist resort of Chongqing. It covers an area of 253 hectares with a floor area of 550,000 square meters. Currently it has 1,600 faculty and staff members and about 24,000 students. CUPT has 15 major departments, 47 undergraduate programs, 38 postgraduate programs and 2 Ph. D programs. The university has been equipped with modern teaching and scientific researching facilities. The local campus network has now connected over10,000 micro-computers.
