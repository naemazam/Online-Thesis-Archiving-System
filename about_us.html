Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CUPT) is an academic-research institution with excellence in the area of information technology. It gives primary emphasis on engineering, also committed to provide professional education in science, management, liberal arts and other areas of its demonstrated competence. The university is located at Nanshan tourist resort of Chongqing. It covers an area of 253 hectares with a floor area of 550,000 square meters. Currently it has 1,600 faculty and staff members and about 24,000 students. CUPT has 15 major departments, 47 undergraduate programs, 38 postgraduate programs and 2 Ph. D programs. The university has been equipped with modern teaching and scientific researching facilities. The local campus network has now connected over10,000 micro-computers. The library has collection of more than 1.6 million books. More information will be found in http://www.cqupt.edu.cn

 

Job Description and Responsibilities:


Candidates should have the ability to teach a variety of classes, such as listening, speaking and writing, western society and culture, newspaper reading and literature. The normal teaching load is 16 hours per week. In addition to teaching, successful candidates will be expected to prepare lessons' correct written assignments and have 1.5 office hours per week consulting students who may have questions on their studies. Successful candidates are also expected to help develop the environment of English language learning in the University. Specifically, each teacher will present one English lecture on topic agreed upon by the relevant departments, and participate in English corners once a week for about 10 weeks in each semester. Teachers may also be asked to judge various English contests, i.e. music, writing and speaking, and to tape record questions for examinations.

 

Qualifications:
Candidates should be native English speakers or have TESOL/TEFL certificate with English teaching experience in China, and have at least a Bachelor degree or equivalent in a relevant subject area. Teaching experience at the degree level and a publication record are preferable. Candidates should be committed to high quality teaching and a willingness to assist students. Canadian Candidates with TESOL/TEFL certificate highly recommended
